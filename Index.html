PERSONAL EXPLOITS | What grounds me?

In the whirlwind of a tech career, where algorithms and code dictate the rhythm of my days, I find solace in the simple, grounding forces that remind me of my humanity. As a South African black woman navigating the corporate world, my personal exploits are not just hobbies; they are lifelines that anchor me amidst the chaos. Writing, for instance, is my sanctuary. In my free time, I weave words into stories that explore the intersections of technology and culture, drawing from my own experiences of breaking barriers in a male-dominated field. These narratives are not mere escapism; they are reflections of resilience, capturing the essence of what it means to thrive against odds.

Art, too, plays a pivotal role in grounding me. Whether it's sketching digital designs or painting vibrant canvases inspired by African motifs, creating visual art allows me to express emotions that code cannot capture. It's a meditative practice, a way to reconnect with my roots and celebrate the beauty of diversity. My weekends often involve community workshops, where I share my skills with young girls aspiring to enter tech, fostering a sense of purpose that extends beyond my professional achievements.

Physical activity is another cornerstone. Running through the streets of Johannesburg at dawn, I feel the pulse of the city mirroring my own heartbeat. It's a reminder of endurance, much like the persistence required in debugging complex software. Yoga sessions in the evening help me unwind, balancing the mental strain of corporate demands with mindfulness. These exploits are not distractions; they are essential for maintaining equilibrium.

Family and cultural traditions also ground me profoundly. Gathering with loved ones for traditional meals or storytelling sessions revives the oral histories that shaped my identity. These moments reaffirm my values, reminding me that success in tech is not just about innovation but about uplifting my community. Through these exploits, I not only recharge but also inspire others. My blog features articles on balancing career and creativity, encouraging women in tech to embrace their multifaceted selves. As I continue to explore, I discover that grounding is not about standing still but about finding stability in motion, much like the ever-evolving world of technology.
