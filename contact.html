CONTACT PREFERENCES | How I Stay Connected

I value meaningful communication and prefer to engage through channels that suit both parties. Email is my primary mode for detailed discussions, where I can respond with thoughtful insights, often within 48 hours. For quick updates or casual chats, Twitter DMs work best, though I moderate my time there due to a busy schedule. LinkedIn is ideal for professional networking—feel free to send a connection request with a note about your interest.

I schedule meetings via a shared calendar link (available upon request), preferring video calls for in-depth collaborations. My availability aligns with South African time zones, typically mornings or late afternoons. I aim to reply promptly but appreciate patience as I balance tech projects and writing. If urgent, a follow-up message is welcome.

Privacy is important to me. I share only necessary details and encourage secure communication. For large projects, I might suggest a preliminary call to outline goals. This approach ensures clarity and fosters productive exchanges, reflecting my dual roles as a tech professional and writer.
