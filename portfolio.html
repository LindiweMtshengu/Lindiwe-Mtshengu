WRITING PROCESS | My Creative Journey

My writing process is a dance between discipline and inspiration. As a tech professional, I often start with late-night coding sessions that spark ideas, which I then translate into prose or poetry. I draw heavily from my South African roots, infusing stories with the rhythms of Johannesburg and the resilience of my community. Drafting happens in bursts—sometimes on my laptop during a quiet weekend, other times in a notebook during a coffee break. Editing is meticulous, where I refine each word to mirror the precision I apply to code.

Inspiration strikes in unexpected places. A conversation with a colleague about AI ethics might evolve into a short story, while a walk through a local market could inspire a poem. I often collaborate with other writers and artists, hosting virtual workshops to exchange ideas. This process not only hones my craft but also connects me to a broader creative network, enriching my narratives with diverse perspectives.

Challenges are part of the journey. Balancing a demanding tech career with writing requires sacrifice—early mornings or late nights are common. Yet, the reward is in seeing my work resonate with readers, especially women in tech who see themselves in my stories. I use tools like Scrivener for organization and Grammarly for polish, but the heart of my writing remains raw and personal.

Looking ahead, I plan to publish a collection of essays on technology’s cultural impact, blending my dual passions. This portfolio is a living document, growing with each piece I craft, reflecting my evolution as both a writer and a tech innovator.
